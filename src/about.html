About Me

With expertise in Python, Laravel, and Flutter, I’ve built a variety of projects, especially focused on enhancing ROI, as I work in a startup dedicated to the publishing industry. I am constantly evolving, embracing new challenges, and expanding my knowledge—currently planning to dive deeper into cybersecurity. Outside of coding, I enjoy reading self-help books to improve my social skills and watching films.

My ultimate goal is to create a fulfilling and honest life, where I can retire my mother and build a family. I also dream of living and working abroad, contributing to global innovation. Just as I hope to contribute to my team, I also look forward to learning and growing alongside them. As Voltaire wisely said, "The more I learn, the more I realize how much I don't know."
